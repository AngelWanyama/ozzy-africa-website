Our Approach

A circular model that connects e-waste recovery, digital skills and real pathways to employment.

Our Strategic Pillars

E-waste Management

We collect, repair, refurbish or safely dismantle electronics to reduce pollution and provide affordable devices to students and small businesses.

Digital Skills Training

Hands-on learning prepares young people for jobs, micro-work and entrepreneurship in the digital economy.

GreenTech & Solar Innovation

Solar-powered hubs reduce electricity costs and ensure stable learning even in low-resource communities.

How Our Model Works

Our circular model creates value at every stage, turning waste into opportunity.

Community Collection Points

Schools, companies and households donate unused electronics to our hub for repair or safe processing.

Repair & Training Labs

Learners gain hands-on repair skills ranging from electronics, ICT, solar and digital creativity.

Job Placement & Micro-Business

Graduates secure meaningful work or start micro-enterprises such as repair services or digital work.
