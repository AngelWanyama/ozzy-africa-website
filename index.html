Who we are

Ozzy Africa Technologies Co. Ltd. is a Ugandan social enterprise registered in 2025 and based in Kampala with active operations in Mbale City. We work at the intersection of digital skills, green innovation, and youth employment.

Mission

Equip young people with digital and green skills while reducing electronic waste.

Vision

A Uganda where every young person accesses technology and earns sustainably.

Goal

Turn waste into opportunity and grow future ready youth.

The problem we address

Three connected challenges affect young people in Uganda.

E-waste pollution

Only 2 percent of Uganda's electronic waste is recycled safely.

Low digital access

Millions of youth leave school without usable computer skills.

Youth unemployment

Over 40 percent of young people are out of work.
