About Us

Building a GreenTech future for young people in Uganda.

Who We Are

Ozzy Africa Technologies is a GreenTech and Circular Innovation enterprise registered in Uganda. We equip young people with practical digital and green skills while reducing electronic waste.

Our solar-powered hub in Mbale City trains young people, provides refurbished devices, and links learners to real employment opportunities.

Registered: 2025
Location: Mbale City, Uganda

Our Story

Ozzy Africa Technologies was founded to respond to three connected challenges affecting young people in Uganda. Digital exclusion, rising electronic waste and lack of meaningful employment opportunities. While these challenges appear separate, in reality they reinforce each other.

We saw young people eager to learn but lacking access to devices. We saw electronic waste increasing with no reuse or repair systems. We saw talented youth unable to earn income because they did not have digital skills or work exposure.

Our model was built to address all three challenges at once. Through a solar-powered GreenTech hub, we collect and repair devices, teach digital and green skills and connect learners to real jobs and entrepreneurship.

What began as a small training effort has now grown into a structured social enterprise serving communities in Mbale City with a bold vision for nationwide impact.
