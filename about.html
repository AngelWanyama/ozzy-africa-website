About Us

Creating a circular, digital and green future for young people in Uganda.

Ozzy Africa Technologies is a Ugandan social enterprise solving three major challenges, electronic waste, digital exclusion and youth unemployment.

Mission

To equip young people, especially women, with digital and green technology skills while reducing e-waste and creating employment pathways.

Vision

A Uganda where every young person can access technology, learn relevant skills and earn a sustainable income through innovation.

Goal

Turn waste into opportunity and prepare youth for stable futures.

Core Values

Inclusion

Opportunities for all regardless of background.

Integrity

Transparent, honest and accountable work.

Sustainability

Promoting repair, reuse and solar-driven innovation.

Community

Working hand in hand with schools and local partners.
