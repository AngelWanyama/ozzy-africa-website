About Ozzy Africa Technologies

Advancing digital and green skills for young people across Uganda

Who We Are

Ozzy Africa Technologies is a registered social enterprise based in Mbale City, Uganda. We strengthen digital and technical skills, expand access to quality training, and promote sustainable e-waste solutions through hands-on repair and innovation.

  • Registered in Uganda in 2024
  • Headquarters in Mbale City
  • Focus areas include digital training, electronic repair, e-waste recovery, and green innovation
  • We support young people, especially young women, aged 15 to 30

Founder’s Message

Ozzy Africa began as a simple idea shaped by lived experience, curiosity, and the belief that young Africans deserve access to the digital world and the opportunities that come with it.

Many young people are left out of the digital economy because they lack access to training, mentorship, and tools. At the same time, e-waste continues to grow in our communities, creating risks but also opportunities for innovation and green skills.

Our work brings these two realities together. We train, repair, recover, and innovate so young people can build better futures while contributing to a cleaner environment.

Angel Wanyama
Founder and Executive Director

Our Mission, Vision, and Goals

Mission

To expand digital and repair skills for young people and reduce e-waste through training, repair, and innovation.

Vision

A future where young people in Uganda thrive in the digital and green economy.

Goals

To strengthen skills, expand access to technology, create jobs, and promote sustainability.

The Problem We Are Solving

Uganda faces three linked challenges. Electronic waste, low digital access, and unemployment. Our work connects these challenges to create practical solutions for young people.
