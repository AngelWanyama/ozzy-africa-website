About Us

Building a GreenTech future for young people in Uganda.

Who We Are

Ozzy Africa Technologies is a GreenTech and Circular Innovation enterprise registered in Uganda. We equip young people with practical digital and green skills while reducing electronic waste.

Our solar-powered hub in Mbale City trains young people, provides refurbished devices, and links learners to real employment opportunities.

Our Work in Pictures

The Problem We Are Solving

Uganda faces rising e-waste pollution, low digital inclusion, and high youth unemployment. Ozzy Africa solves these challenges through one integrated model.

Founder’s Message

Angel Wanyama

Ozzy Africa was created to bridge the gap between waste, opportunity, and unemployment. We believe young people deserve access to tools, digital skills, and a dignified path to income.
