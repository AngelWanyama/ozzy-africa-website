About Ozzy Africa Technologies

Advancing digital and green skills for young people through practical training, e-waste management, and sustainable innovation.

Who We Are

Ozzy Africa Technologies is a registered social enterprise based in Mbale City, Uganda. We focus on digital skilling, electronic repair, e-waste management, and creating pathways for work and innovation for young people.

We began as a small repair and reuse initiative and have since grown into a full training and technology hub serving communities across Eastern Uganda.

  • Registered in Uganda in 2024
  • Headquarters in Mbale City
  • Primary beneficiaries are young women and youth aged 15 to 30
  • Focus areas include digital skills, e-waste solutions, and green innovation

Our Mission, Vision, and Goals

Our Mission

To expand digital and repair skills for young people, strengthen access to technology, and reduce electronic waste across Uganda.

Our Vision

A future where every young person in Uganda can access digital skills and sustainable technology opportunities.

Our Goals

To create jobs, reduce digital exclusion, promote green innovation, and strengthen local technical capacity through hands-on training.
